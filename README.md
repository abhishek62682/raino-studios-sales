## Our Website Services

Raino Studios offers **two types of websites** based on the client's requirements and budget:

| Type | Description |
|---|---|
| Template-Based Website | Fast, cost-effective, built on customized templates |
| Custom Website | Fully bespoke design & development from scratch |

---

### 1. Template-Based Website

A fast and cost-effective website solution built using professionally designed templates that are customized to match the client's brand.

**Best For**
- Salons
- Clinics
- Doctors
- Restaurants
- Cafés
- Gyms
- Fitness Centers
- Small Businesses
- Startups

**Process**
1. Understand the client's business and requirements.
2. Share multiple professionally designed templates for the client to choose from.
3. Customize the selected template with:
   - Company Logo
   - Brand Colors
   - Images
   - Content
   - Contact Information
4. Review the website with the client.
5. Make minor revisions if required.
6. Test the website.
7. Deploy the website on the client's domain.

### 2. Custom Website

A completely unique website designed and developed from scratch according to the client's business goals and requirements. Every design is created specifically for the client — nothing is copied from templates.

**Best For**
- Premium Brands
- Clinics
- Architects
- Interior Designers
- Real Estate
- Wedding Photographers
- Hotels
- Businesses that want a premium online presence
- Businesses requiring custom features

**Process**
1. Understand the client's business, goals, competitors, and requirements.
2. Create a completely custom UI/UX design in Figma.
3. Present the design to the client for review and approval.
4. Revise the design based on client feedback.
5. Once the design is approved, begin website development.
6. Test the website across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
7. Deploy the website on the client's domain.

## How to Recommend the Right Website

**Recommend a Template-Based Website when:**
- The client has a limited budget.
- The client needs a website quickly.
- They only require standard business pages.
- They don't require custom functionality.

**Recommend a Custom Website when:**
- The client wants a premium online presence.
- The client wants a unique design.
- They require custom functionality.
- They want the website to match their branding.
- They see the website as a long-term investment.
